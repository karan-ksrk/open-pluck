Open Pluck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Open Pluck is a Chrome extension that lets you highlight text in ChatGPT or Claude.ai conversations and ask follow-up questions in a separate popup window. Your privacy matters to us.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension does not send any information to our servers or any third-party servers. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no external API calls made by the extension.

Local Storage

Open Pluck uses Chrome's chrome.storage.local API only to:

  • Store your selected AI service (ChatGPT or Claude.ai) as a preference
  • Temporarily pass your highlighted text and question to the popup window when you ask a follow-up

This data stays entirely on your device. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere. The temporary question data is cleared after it is used to submit your question to the AI service.

How Your Questions Are Submitted

When you ask a follow-up question, the extension opens a new browser window to ChatGPT or Claude.ai and submits your question through the normal web interface using your existing logged-in session. The extension does not use any API keys or make requests on your behalf to external servers. All communication goes directly between your browser and the AI service you are already using.

Permissions

We request the following permissions:

  • storage — To save your service preference and temporarily pass your question to the popup
  • windows — To open the popup window where your question is answered
  • tabs — To manage the popup window
  • chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com, claude.ai — To inject the highlight popover and submit questions on these sites only

We do not access any other websites or your browsing history.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
