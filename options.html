Uses your existing ChatGPT or Claude.ai browser session. Nothing to pay, nothing to configure.

Answer Service
Choose which AI answers your questions. You must be logged in to that service in your browser.
How to use
1
Open ChatGPT or Claude.ai and have any conversation.
2
Highlight any text inside an AI response with your mouse.
3
A popover appears. Click a quick question or type a custom one.
4
A small popup window opens and auto-submits your question to ChatGPT or Claude. The answer appears there — your main conversation is untouched.
5
The sidebar on the right tracks all your questions for the session. Click "Focus chat window" to jump back to the answer anytime.

Make sure you're logged in to ChatGPT and/or Claude.ai before using HighlightAsk. The extension uses your existing browser session.

Privacy: No data is collected. Your questions go directly to ChatGPT or Claude through your own logged-in session. Nothing is stored externally.
